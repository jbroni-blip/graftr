Common questions

What does the 14-day trial include?
You get full Crew access for 14 days — unlimited AI, pricing intelligence, auto-chase, everything. No card required to start. At the end you pick a tier or drop to Solo. Nothing gets deleted.
What's the Stripe fee and can I avoid it?
Card payments go through Stripe. Their standard UK rate is 1.4% + 20p per transaction. We add a small clip on top — 0.6% on Solo, 0.3% on Crew, 0% on Yard. If you prefer BACS transfers there's no fee at all.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from Settings at any time. Annual plans get a pro-rata refund on the unused portion, no questions. Your data — every quote, invoice and photo — exports as PDF and CSV on the way out. We'll never hold it hostage.
How does the pricing intelligence work?
Every quote sent through Graftr (anonymised, never identifiable) feeds a UK-wide dataset. Graftr AI uses this to tell you what builders in your area are actually winning jobs at — not guesswork, real market data. Available on Crew and Yard.
Do you do white-label?
Yard tier includes a fully white-label customer portal — your branding on quotes, invoices and the payment page. Custom domain (e.g. quotes.yourbusiness.co.uk) included at no extra cost.
