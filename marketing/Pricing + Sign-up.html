Common questions

What does "free forever" actually mean?
Solo really is free for life. No credit card, no clock ticking, no ads. You get 5 voice quotes, 3 photo quotes, unlimited customers, and Stripe pay-links (at standard 2% fee) on every invoice. Most sole traders never need more.
What's the Stripe fee thing?
Card payments go through Stripe. Their normal UK rate is 1.5% + 20p. We take a small cut on top — 0.5% on Solo, 0.2% on Crew, 0% on Yard — to keep the app free / cheap. If you'd rather take BACS, no fee, no problem.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Crew and Yard cancel cleanly from Settings. We refund the unused portion of any annual plan, pro-rata, no questions. We'll never lock your data — every quote, invoice and photo exports as PDF + JSON on the way out.
What about my Tradify / Jobber / Powered Now data?
Export it from your old tool (usually a CSV or PDF). Graftr imports customers, active quotes, open invoices, and the last 12 months of jobs. Takes 8–14 minutes typically. We keep your old account intact until you say so — switching is reversible.
Do you do white-label?
Yard tier includes a white-label customer portal — your branding on quotes, invoices and the payment page. Custom domain (e.g. quotes.rawlingsbuilds.co.uk) included.
How does Graftr AI handle my data?
AI is trained on aggregate, anonymised UK trade data (240k jobs) — never your specific job details unless you opt in. Your customers' details never leave your account. You can switch off "AI sees my jobs" in Settings.
What if I employ subbies?
Crew lets you bring on up to 2 subs with their own Graftr login (read-only or "log photos & hours"). CIS deductions, statements, P32 — all automatic. Yard removes the cap and adds full payroll.
Can I get an invoice for my accountant?
Of course. Every Graftr subscription gets a proper VAT invoice each month. Or annual — your choice. Comes with a CSV/PDF expense breakdown if you want it line-by-line.
