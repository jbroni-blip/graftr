Graftr is the quoting, invoicing and AI copilot for UK tradespeople. Built for builders, sparkies, plumbers, chippies, decorators and everyone in between — by people who've been on the tools and got tired of doing the books on a Sunday night.

  • 90-second voice quotes. Speak the brief, get a priced quote your customer can sign off in a tap.
  • Photo quotes. Snap the job, get a drafted scope & price in 8 seconds — trained on 240k UK jobs.
  • Pay-as-you-go links. Stripe-powered card & Apple Pay on every invoice. Get paid same day.
  • AI copilot for trades. Knows UK prices, regs, your past jobs, and the materials you used last time.
  • Diary & job site. Drag jobs into the week. Photo log, variation sign-off, snag list — all in one.
  • Subbie mode. Pay the lads, track hours, CIS-ready statements ready for HMRC.
  • Free forever. Five quotes a month, unlimited if you take payments via Graftr (2% fee, vs Stripe's 1.5%).
