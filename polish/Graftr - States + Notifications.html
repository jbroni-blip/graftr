3 rails: Money (urgent · always permitted) · Site (high-signal) · Coach (AI insights, can mute). Hard daily cap of 6.
Push system · rules how we don't annoy
// THE THREE RAILS
Money · always-on
Payment landed, payment failed, deposit cleared, chase delivered. Unmuteable — these are why Graftr exists.
CAP · 8/DAY · MEDIAN 2
Site · high-signal
Customer replied, variation needs sign-off, daily site brief, crew clocked off. Muteable individually.
CAP · 4/DAY · MEDIAN 2
Coach · AI insights
"You're under-pricing", "your best week", "3 chases ready". Off by default after first 4 weeks if untapped.
CAP · 2/DAY · OPT-OUT AT WK 4
// FORMAT RULES
  • ✓ Title says what happened, body says so what.
  • ✓ Names not pronouns ("Tom", not "your customer").
  • ✓ Numbers up-front (£15,195, not "a payment").
  • ✓ Quiet hours · 21:00–07:00 (override only for Money).
  • ✓ Saturday → Coach off · Sunday → all off (unless overridden).
Tradespeople hate being spammed by the boring office app. These rules keep us welcome on the lock screen.
